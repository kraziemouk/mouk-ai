MSP-To-Internal Transition Approach

  1. Discover: review MSP scope, SLAs, escalation paths, employee pain points, and undocumented dependencies.
  2. Stabilize: preserve continuity, clarify ownership, and create visibility into workflows, assets, access, and vendor performance.
  3. Internalize: prioritize high-value functions, build knowledge base and runbooks, and hire around operational gaps.
  4. Optimize: standardize tooling, improve automation, mature security posture, and establish scalable service metrics.
